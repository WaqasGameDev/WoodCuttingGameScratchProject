-This project is made in MIT Scracth Game Engine
-Click play button.
-The axe and red cross will start to move.
-When red cross is on Cyan mark (Wood Cutting Mark), press space bar, the axe will cut the wood.
-When whole tree will be cut into wood, mission will be complete.
-If axe hits the area other than the cutting mark, you will lose one try. Lose all three tries and game will be over.
-Enjoy!
